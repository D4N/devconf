FAQ

When and where is next DevConf.cz event?

Friday to Sunday January 26-28, 2018 at the beautiful Faculty of Information Technology (VUT FIT) on Božetěchova 2 in Brno, Czechia.

Can I submit a proposal?

YES. Submit your proposals at cfp.devconf.cz.

When does the CfP open & close?

CfP opens September 15 and closes November 17, 2017

When will speakers be confirmed?

Speakers will receive acceptance/rejection notifications by December 1, 2017.

When will the schedule / event program be published?

The DevConf.cz 2018 event schedule will be published on-line by December 22, 2017

Can I host a community booth or meetup?

YES. This is a community contributors conference. If you'd like to reserve some booth space to host (for example) interactive demos or organize a community meet-up, submit your request using the DevConf CfP page over at cfp.devconf.cz. Limited capacity!

Is registration required?

YES. {{ _('On-line registration coming soon! Limited capacity!') }} {{ _('On-site (at venue) registration is possible, even after on-line regitration has closed! But it will also be limited capacity. On-line registration is preferred.') }}

Is registration really FREE?

YES.

Can I request a visa invitation letter?

YES, but only for accepted speakers. We don't assure it will help, and we can't offer any more assistance than this. Invitation letter request information will be announced once registration is open. Stay tuned.

Will there be refreshments available?

YES. Accepted speakers are expected to get some amount of 'meal vouchers' for use during the event. Otherwise, multiple food options will be available for folks to purchase on-site, as well.

Can I request financial aide?

NO. Sorry, but this is a free event. All participants will need to cover their own costs. With the only exception being that accepted speakers also have an option to stay at our of our preferred hotels for free for up to three nights! Booking instructions will be sent along with your accepted confirmation.

Can my company sponsor the event?

NO. We are not looking for any additional sponsors at this time.
